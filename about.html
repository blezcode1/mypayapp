ABOUT US

Flatpay is financial institution which accept payment across web and mobile platform, allow student to forward payment to their institution with less charge and checks, generate receipt accepted by educational institutions. Flatpay comes with a wholesome benefits for student to help them have a stress-free payment experience. With flatpay you can save up your school fee over a certain period and pay it when it complete, you also get to schedule when to pay your school fees and we also help to get rid of documentation stress.







OUR MISSION

Mission Our core values is to enable students gain access to school's paying portal through direct bank payment in web or in app without checks or charges through automatic transfers from students FLAT PAY account to school's paying portal to help ease long queues at the bank and at the busar's office.

OUR VISION

Our vision. To become Nigeria's leading high-quality financial aid to deliver solutions to problems associated with students financial data and transform school's services.
