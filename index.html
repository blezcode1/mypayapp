ABOUT US

FlatPay is a financial institution which accepts payment accross web and mobile platform, allows students to forward payment to their institution without charges and checks, generate receipt accepted by educational institutions.

...

FlatPay comes with a wholesome benefit for students to help them have a stress free payment experience. With FlatPay, you can save up your school fees over a certain period and pay it when it is complete. You also get to schedule when to pay your school fees and we also help to get rid of documentation stress.

FlatPay is for all school payments.
